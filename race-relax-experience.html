Race - Relax - Repeat

LOCATION Abu Dhabi
DATES 3 Dec - 7 Dec '26
FROM 100

Once a year, Yas Marina Circuit becomes the centre of the motorsport world, and this trip puts you right at the heart of it. Four nights based at Hotel Taj Jumeirah Lake Towers in Dubai, with reserved seating at the South Grandstand, Turn 9, exactly where the racing gets close, loud, and unmissable.

Beyond the racing itself, the weekend is built around everything that makes an Abu Dhabi Grand Prix unlike any other stop on the calendar: after-race concerts under the lights at Etihad Park every night from Thursday to Sunday, a Thursday pit lane walk, driver fan-stage appearances, and full access to the F1 Fanzone and Oasis areas. our ticket also includes one day's entry to a single Yas Island theme park of your choice, Ferrari World, Yas Waterworld, Warner Bros. World, SeaWorld, or Team Lab Phenomena, so there's a reason to extend the magic beyond the circuit itself.

Back at base, the Taj Jumeirah Lake Towers offers an infinity rooftop pool, a renowned spa, and two standout restaurants in Paros and Shamiana, all just a 60-minute transfer from the action and close to Dubai Marina Mall and Marina Beach for downtime between sessions.

From a welcome dinner on arrival to a late-night farewell dinner after the chequered flag falls, your dedicated Atlas Experience representative is with you throughout, handling every transfer and detail so all that's left to do is soak in the weekend.

Day by day

Itinerary

03 DEC

Arrival & Welcome Dinner

03 DEC
  • Arrive in Dubai and check in to the Taj Jumeirah Lake Towers, your five-star home base for the entire weekend
  • Afternoon at leisure to explore Dubai, the Marina, or simply settle in after travel
  • Group welcome dinner and Atlas Experience briefing, introducing the group and the weekend ahead
04 DEC

Practice Day

04 DEC
  • Transfer to Yas Marina Circuit (around 60 minutes) for your first day at the South Grandstand, Turn 9, the Marsa Corner hairpin where the high-speed DRS straight gives way to side-by-side battles
  • Watch FP1 and FP2 from the grandstand, or use your included 1-day theme park access to explore Yas Island instead, Ferrari World, Yas Waterworld, Warner Bros. World, SeaWorld, or Team Lab Phenomena; the F1 Fanzone and Oasis areas are open throughout for those at the circuit
  • Stay for the after-race concert at Etihad Park before returning to Dubai
05 DEC

Qualifying Day

05 DEC
  • Transfer to Yas Marina Circuit to watch Q1, Q2, and Q3 unfold from the South Grandstand, with panoramic views across Yas Marina and the W Abu Dhabi hotel
  • Soak in the atmosphere of driver fan-stage appearances and the wider Grand Prix weekend energy
  • Stay for the after-qualifying concert at Etihad Park before returning to Dubai
06 DEC

Race Day & Farewell Dinner

06 DEC
  • Transfer to Yas Marina Circuit for the main event, the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix itself, watched from reserved covered seat-back seating with a giant screen in sight for full race coverage
  • Stay for the post-race concert at Etihad Park, the perfect high-energy close to race weekend
  • Late-night farewell dinner around midnight, celebrating an unforgettable weekend with the group
07 DEC

Departure

07 DEC
  • Final breakfast at the Taj Jumeirah Lake Towers and check-out
  • Airport transfer and Departure. Return home having experienced Formula 1's season finale from the heart of the action, with memories of world-class racing, live entertainment, and unforgettable moments both on and off the track
