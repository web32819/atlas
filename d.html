DAY BY DAY

Itinerary

17 JUL

Arrival + Waterfront Evening

  • Arrive in Split and settle in at Le Méridien Lav Split; relax by the pool or beach
  • Take a gentle walk along the Riva Promenade to get a first feel of the city
  • Dinner at a casual seafood restaurant along the promenade

Tennis + Old Town + Marjan Hill

  • Morning tennis session with a professional coach
  • Explore the historic Old Town of Split
  • Sunset hike up Marjan Hill with panoramic views

Tennis + Half-Day Island Boat Trip

  • Early morning tennis drills and match play
  • Private boat trip to the nearby islands
  • Swimming, snorkelling, and fresh seafood lunch on board
20 JUL

Tennis + Culture & Departure

  • Final morning tennis session and farewell breakfast
  • Visit Diocletian's Palace before heading to the airport
