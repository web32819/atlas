Phuket - Ocean Adventure

LOCATION Phuket
DATES 9 Feb - 13 Feb '27
FROM 100

Phuket delivers the perfect blend of adrenaline and island ease, based at the stunning Hotel Wyndham Grand Phuket Kalim Bay (or similar category), perched above one of the island's most scenic bays with direct access to the water.

Each day brings a different discipline: from the underwater world of scuba and the breath-hold focus of freediving, to the raw energy of surfing Phuket's breaks.

The final day slows the pace right down, with a sunset cruise and a farewell dinner to close out the trip in style.

Designed for travellers who want immersion without routine, this short escape blends ocean adventure, island culture, and pure relaxation in equal measure.

Day by day

Itinerary

09 FEB

Arrival + Evening in Kalim Bay

09 FEB
  • Arrive in Phuket and settle in at Wyndham Grand Phuket Kalim Bay (or similar category); take in the sweeping bay views and unwind after your journey
  • Welcome dinner at a restaurant in Patong, where your Atlas Experience representative will introduce the group and walk through the week ahead
10 FEB

Scuba Diving

10 FEB
  • Full guided scuba diving excursion to one of Phuket's finest dive sites, known for leopard sharks, vivid soft corals, and excellent Andaman visibility
  • Briefing and full equipment provided; PADI-qualified instructors on hand throughout, suitable for certified divers and first-timers alike
  • Return to the hotel for a relaxed evening; dinner at a local Thai restaurant in Phuket Town, known for its creative cuisine and warm, unpretentious atmosphere
11 FEB

Freediving

11 FEB
  • Morning freediving session with a certified instructor at one of Phuket's calmest and clearest bays, ideal for breath-hold training, equalisation technique, and relaxed water entry
  • A meditative contrast to scuba, requiring stillness, focus, and trust in your own body; suitable for complete beginners with no prior experience needed
  • Enjoy authentic street food at a traditional Thai night market
12 FEB

Surfing

12 FEB
  • Guided surf session at Kata Beach, Phuket's most consistent break for all levels, with warm water, steady swell, and professional instructors on hand to coach technique and build confidence
  • Afternoon visit to the Big Buddha, a 45-metre white marble landmark on Nakkerd Hill with sweeping views across the island, followed by a wander through the vibrant stalls and temples nearby
  • Dinner at a beachside restaurant in Kata or Karon, relaxed, informal, and the perfect setting to swap surf stories
13 FEB

Leisure + Sunset Cruise + Farewell Dinner

13 FEB
  • Morning at leisure followed by a traditional Thai massage at one of Phuket's renowned spas, a well-earned wind-down after four active days on the water
  • Board a private sunset cruise along the Andaman coastline, drifting past limestone karsts and quiet coves as the sky turns gold, with drinks and canapés on deck
  • Group farewell dinner at a waterfront restaurant, celebrating five days of adventure, new friendships, and the Phuket experience
