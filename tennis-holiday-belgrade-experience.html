Belgrade - Tennis Holiday

LOCATION Belgrade
DATES 29 Apr - 3 May '27
FROM $1,549

Belgrade is one of Europe's most exciting and underrated city destinations, a city of raw energy, extraordinary food, legendary nightlife, and a sporting culture that runs deep. Serbia has produced some of the greatest players the game has ever seen, and this trip puts you directly inside that world.

Train across three of Belgrade's leading tennis academies: Serbia Tennis Academy, known for its structured, performance-driven coaching system; Tennis Academy ADA, led by Bogdan Obradovic, former Serbian Davis Cup-winning captain and long-time coach to Janko Tipsarevic; and the Tipsarevic Tennis Academy itself, founded by former World No. 8 Janko Tipsarevic and the training ground for ATP professionals. Over three days you'll combine focused coaching with competitive match play, sharpening your game across three distinct coaching philosophies, all from the base of the iconic Metropol Palace Hotel (or similar category), one of Belgrade's most storied five-star addresses.

Between sessions, Belgrade reveals itself, a city that surprises every visitor who arrives not quite knowing what to expect, and leaves every one of them wanting to come back.

Designed for tennis players who want to train seriously, compete hard, and experience a city that nobody back home has done quite like this.

Day by day

Itinerary

29 APR

Arrival in Belgrade

29 APR
  • Arrive in Belgrade and check in to Metropol Palace Hotel (or similar category), a landmark five-star address in the heart of the city, combining grand heritage architecture with contemporary luxury and exceptional service
  • Take an evening stroll through Kalemegdan Fortress, perched dramatically above the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers, a stunning first impression of the city at golden hour
  • Welcome dinner with your Atlas Experience representative, where the group is introduced and the week ahead is walked through over good wine and Serbian hospitality
30 APR

Coaching Day 1: Serbia Tennis Academy

30 APR
  • Morning coaching session at Serbia Tennis Academy, working with their structured coaching system; focus on technical foundations, footwork, and shot consistency
  • Afternoon match play session, applying the morning's coaching in structured competitive play, with feedback from the coaching team between games
  • Evening in Skadarlija, Belgrade's bohemian cobbled quarter of traditional kafanas, live music, and grilled meats; one of the most atmospheric streets in the city
1 MAY

Coaching Day 2: Tennis Academy ADA - Bogdan Obradovic

1 MAY
  • Morning coaching session at Tennis Academy ADA on Ada Ciganlija, set in one of Belgrade's most peaceful green spaces; by Bogdan Obradovic, former Serbian Davis Cup-winning captain and long-time coach to Janko Tipsarevic, with sessions focused on tactical play and match intelligence
  • Afternoon match play session, competitive, mini-tournament format to sharpen instincts and put the morning's work under real pressure
  • Evening drinks and dinner on the Sava riverbank, Belgrade's famous floating bar and restaurant scene, with the city lights reflecting on the water
2 MAY

Coaching Day 3: Tipsarevic Tennis Academy + Farewell

2 MAY
  • Morning coaching session at Tipsarevic Tennis Academy, founded by former ATP World No. 8 Janko Tipsarevic and the training ground for some of Serbia's finest professionals; train on the same courts used by touring players, with a closing debrief and personalised development plan to take home
  • Afternoon tournament, full competitive match play among the group
  • Farewell dinner at one of Belgrade's celebrated restaurants, celebrating days of coaching, competitive tennis, and a city that delivered far more than expected
3 MAY

Departure

3 MAY
  • Check out of Metropol Palace Hotel (or similar category) and transfer to Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport, taking home a sharper game, new friendships, and a city that will stay with you long after you land.
