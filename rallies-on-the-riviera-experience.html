Rallies On The Riviera

LOCATION Split, Croatia
DATES 17 Jul - 20 Jul '26
FROM $2,999

Spend a few days in Split based at Le Méridien Lav Split, where on-site tennis courts make it easy to start each day with focused 2.5-hour sessions.

From there, discover the historic streets of Diocletian’s Palace, walk along the lively Riva Promenade, and take in coastal views from Marjan Hill.

One afternoon shifts things onto the water, with a boat trip to nearby islands like Hvar or Brač—expect swim stops, clear blue water, and a refreshing change of pace before returning to shore.

Designed for travellers who want structure without feeling over-planned, this short escape blends tennis, light exploration, and relaxed evenings by the sea.
