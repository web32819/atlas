Our Philosophy

The Atlas Experience is a curated travel company focused on small-group journeys built around specific passions, from tennis and padel to diving, motorsport, chess, and the world's most extraordinary food and cultural festivals. And if there's something else on your list, an overnight camp on the edge of Trolltunga in Norway, 2am gadgets shopping at Mustafa in Singapore, a sleepover under the stars in Wadi Rum, chances are we've already done it, or we'll find a way to make it happen.

  • We create structured, well-designed experiences for individuals who don’t want generic travel packages, but also don’t want to plan everything themselves.

  • Every trip is built around its own unique experiences, no two itineraries are alike, each one shaped entirely around the passion at its heart.

  • Travelling as a group? We also build fully custom trips for friends, families, and private groups, designed entirely around what your group wants to do.

  • Our trips are intentionally limited in size. Every experience is designed to go beyond access and into immersion.

HOW

It Began

1

The Atlas Experience was born from a single belief: travel is most meaningful when shared with people who truly understand your passions.

2

That belief came from personal experience — years of exploring the world, from free diving in crystal-clear waters to watching Formula 1 trackside, always wondering: where are the people who love this as much as I do?

3

Great experiences existed. Great travel communities existed. Bringing them together was the opportunity no one had seized yet.

4

So I built something different — a new kind of journey where you explore the world, live your passions, and travel alongside people who genuinely get it.
